Understanding the Math (Optional):

For the mathematically curious, here's a simplified version of the EMI formula used by our calculator:

EMI = [ P x R x (1+R)^T ] / [ (1+R)^T - 1]

Where:

  • P = Loan Principal Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Interest Rate / 12)
  • T = Loan Tenure in Months
